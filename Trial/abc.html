About The Course

Our "Mentoring of School Children" program connects enthusiastic college students with young, curious minds. We offer engaging sessions in subjects like coding, robotics, science experiments, and much more. Our goal is to foster a love for learning, build essential skills, and inspire the next generation of innovators.
